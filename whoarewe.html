Our Story.

Born out of the desire to go back to our natural roots, Tyler & Lye Soap Co. was established in 2025, inspired by many other pioneers of soap-making. We found ourselves tired of dried and irritated skin cause from “whatever that brand is.”

This is why our team devised a plan to make high-grade quality soaps, with its raw materials originating from trusted clinics, offering customers ease of mind with whatever they are applying on their body.
